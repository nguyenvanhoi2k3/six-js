What is six-js? A small animation engine (GSAP-inspired) plus a handful of independent, framework-agnostic web components like this accordion. Does it support keyboard navigation? Yes - Enter/Space toggles the focused trigger, and ArrowUp/ArrowDown/Home/End move focus between triggers, matching the WAI-ARIA Accordion pattern. Can I close every item? In single-select mode, yes - clicking the currently open item's trigger again collapses it, leaving nothing open. This item is disabled You should never be able to focus or open this one.
