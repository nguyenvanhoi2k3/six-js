svgMotion × onScroll

Scroll down. Each section below wires an SvgMotion (draw/path mode) animation to OnScroll manually - SvgMotion doesn't have a built-in onScroll shorthand the way six.to() does, so you call OnScroll.create({ animation, trigger, ... }) yourself.

↓ keep scrolling ↓

1. draw — toggle reveal on scroll-in

Default toggle behavior (no sync): crossing into range going forward calls .play() once. Scrolling back up does nothing by default (matches GSAP's own "play none none none").

↓

2. draw — scrubbed reveal (tied 1:1 to scroll position)

sync: true sets progress directly from scroll fraction across the whole (tall) section - scroll up and it un-draws, exactly in sync, no easing/duration lag.

↓

3. draw — staggered multi-shape reveal on scroll-in

A whole word (3 separate paths) drawn in with stagger, all wired to the same trigger.

↓

4. motionPath — scrubbed flight along the whole section

sync: 0.3 (smoothed scrub) drives motionPath's progress from scroll position - the plane's exact spot on the curve tracks how far you've scrolled through this section.
